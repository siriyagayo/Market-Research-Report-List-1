<p><p>The pressure bearing market is categorized into low, medium, and high-speed bearings based on operational speed and application. Low-speed bearings are designed for heavy loads at lower RPMs, often used in construction and mining equipment. Medium-speed bearings support moderate RPMs, suitable for applications like automotive and manufacturing. High-speed bearings operate at elevated RPMs, essential for industries such as aerospace and robotics, where precision and durability are critical. Each type addresses specific performance requirements and operational environments.</p></p>
