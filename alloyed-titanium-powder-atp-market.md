<p><p>Alloyed Titanium Powder (ATP) encompasses various grades tailored for specific applications, including Ti-101, Ti-201, and Ti-301. Ti-101 is characterized by its excellent strength-to-weight ratio, making it suitable for aerospace applications. Ti-201 provides enhanced corrosion resistance, ideal for chemical processing industries. Ti-301 is known for its superior biocompatibility, commonly used in medical implants. The demand for these alloyed powders is driven by advancements in additive manufacturing, aerospace, automotive, and biomedical sectors, highlighting their diverse utility across industries.</p></p>
