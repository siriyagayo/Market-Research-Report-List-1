<p><strong>The Automatic Flexographic Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unit -type Machine</li><li>Central Impression Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Flexographic Printer market is primarily divided into two types: Unit-type machines and Central Impression Machines (CIM). Unit-type machines consist of individual printing units aligned in series, allowing for flexibility in printing different designs and colors. In contrast, Central Impression Machines feature a large central drum that holds all printing plates, enabling high-speed, high-quality printing with reduced setup times. Both types cater to diverse printing needs in packaging, labels, and other applications, supporting the growing demand for efficient and versatile printing solutions.</p></p>
