<p><p>The Transformer Online Conditioning Monitoring market encompasses various techniques for assessing transformer health. Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA) detects gas quantities to indicate issues like insulation breakdown. Partial Discharge (PD) monitoring identifies localized insulation failures. Bushing Monitoring evaluates the condition of transformer bushings. Through Fault Current monitoring assesses the response to electrical faults. Integrated Online Monitoring combines multiple techniques, offering a comprehensive view of transformer performance, enhancing reliability and enabling proactive maintenance strategies to mitigate potential failures.</p></p>
