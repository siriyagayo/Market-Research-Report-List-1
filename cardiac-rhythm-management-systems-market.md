<p><p>Cardiac Rhythm Management Systems encompass various devices designed to manage heart rhythm disorders. Heart Failure (HF) Management Systems focus on monitoring and treating heart failure patients. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) Devices improve heart function through synchronized contractions. Implantable Cardiac Defibrillators (ICDs) detect and correct life-threatening arrhythmias, providing shocks when necessary to restore normal rhythm. Pacemakers regulate heartbeats by delivering electrical pulses, ensuring an appropriate heart rate in patients with bradycardia or other rhythm abnormalities.</p></p>
