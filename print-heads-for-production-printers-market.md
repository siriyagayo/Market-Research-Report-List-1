<p><strong>The Print Heads for Production Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Print Heads</li><li>Thermal Print Heads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Print heads for production printers are crucial components that determine print quality and speed. The market primarily consists of two types: piezoelectric and thermal print heads. </p><p>Piezoelectric print heads utilize an electrical charge to create precise droplets of ink, allowing for higher resolution and a wider range of ink types, including UV and solvent inks. In contrast, thermal print heads heat the ink to create vapor bubbles that force droplets onto the media, making them ideal for standard dye and pigment inks, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity.</p></p>
