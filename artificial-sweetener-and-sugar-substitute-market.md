<p><p>The artificial sweetener and sugar substitute market is primarily categorized into two types: low-intensity sweeteners (LIS) and high-intensity sweeteners (HIS). LIS, such as sorbitol and xylitol, provide a mild sweetness and are often used in products targeting health-conscious consumers. HIS, including aspartame and steviol glycosides, are significantly sweeter than sugar, allowing for smaller quantities to achieve desired sweetness levels. Both categories cater to various dietary needs and preferences, including low-calorie and sugar-free options, thereby expanding market opportunities.</p></p>
