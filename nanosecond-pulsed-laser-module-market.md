<p><ul><li>Solid Nanosecond Laser</li><li>Fiber Nanosecond Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanosecond pulsed laser module market is primarily segmented into two types: solid nanosecond lasers and fiber nanosecond lasers. Solid nanosecond lasers utilize solid-state gain media, delivering high peak power and pulse energy, making them suitable for applications like material processing and medical procedures. In contrast, fiber nanosecond lasers leverage optical fibers as the gain medium, offering high efficiency, compactness, and excellent beam quality, ideal for telecommunications, industrial laser machining, and sensing applications, driving innovation in various sectors.</p></p>
