<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanoindentation measurement device market can be categorized into static and dynamic types. Static nanoindentation focuses on measuring material hardness and mechanical properties under a constant load, providing insights into material behavior at rest. In contrast, dynamic nanoindentation employs varying load conditions to assess viscoelastic properties and time-dependent responses of materials. Both market types cater to diverse applications, including materials science, coatings, and biomaterials, enabling researchers to gain a comprehensive understanding of material characteristics across different conditions.</p></p>
