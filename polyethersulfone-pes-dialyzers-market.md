<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyethersulfone (PES) dialyzers market is categorized into two main types: low flux membranes and high flux membranes. Low flux membranes are designed to filter small molecules and are typically used for patients with stable renal function. In contrast, high flux membranes allow for the removal of larger molecules and toxins, making them suitable for patients with more severe conditions. Both types are essential for improving patient outcomes in dialysis treatments, catering to varying clinical needs and health conditions.</p></p>
