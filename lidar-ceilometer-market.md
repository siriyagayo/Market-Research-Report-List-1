<p><p>A Lidar ceilometer is a sophisticated optical remote sensing instrument used to measure the height of clouds, aerosols, and other atmospheric components. It employs laser technology to emit light pulses that bounce off particles in the atmosphere, allowing accurate assessment of cloud base heights and atmospheric conditions. These devices are crucial in meteorology, aviation safety, and air quality monitoring.</p><p>The Lidar ceilometer market is projected to experience significant growth, driven by increased demand for accurate weather forecasting and real-time atmospheric data.
