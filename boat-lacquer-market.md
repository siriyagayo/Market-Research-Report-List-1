<p><ul><li>1-Component Boat Lacquer</li><li>2-Component Boat Lacquer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat lacquer market is divided into two main types: 1-Component and 2-Component formulations. </p><p>1-Component boat lacquer is a single-package system that requires no mixing, making it user-friendly and ideal for quick applications. It typically offers adequate protection and a glossy finish suitable for light-duty marine environments.</p><p>2-Component boat lacquer, on the other hand, consists of two parts that must be mixed before application. This type provides superior durability, chemical resistance, and a longer-lasting finish, making it suitable for high-performance marine applications.</p></p>
