Powder Electrostatic Spray Guns utilize dry powder coatings that create a thicker finish and are ideal for durable surfaces, while Liquid Electrostatic Spray Guns apply liquid paints or coatings, allowing for smoother finishes and a wider range of color options. Both technologies enhance coating efficiency, reduce material waste, and improve adhesion, making them popular in various industrial applications.</p></p>
