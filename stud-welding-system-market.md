<p><strong>The Stud Welding System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drawn Arc Stud Welding</li><li>Capacitor Discharge Stud Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stud welding system market is primarily divided into two types: Drawn Arc Stud Welding and Capacitor Discharge Stud Welding. Drawn Arc Stud Welding uses an electric arc generated between the stud and workpiece to fuse them, suitable for heavy materials and larger studs. Capacitor Discharge Stud Welding, on the other hand, employs stored electrical energy to rapidly weld smaller studs to thin materials, offering speed and efficiency. Both methods cater to different applications and material thickness requirements in construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
