<p><p>The coating thickness measurement instruments market includes several key types, each suited for specific applications. Magnetic coating thickness gauges measure non-destructive coating on ferromagnetic substrates. Eddy current instruments assess non-magnetic coatings on conductive materials. X-ray thickness gauges provide precise measurements for a variety of coatings, including those on complex shapes, while ultrasonic instruments utilize sound waves to measure thickness in non-conductive coatings on any substrate. Each technology offers unique advantages, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
