<p><p>The freeze drying equipment market includes several types tailored for diverse applications. Tray style freeze dryers utilize flat trays for efficient drying of bulk materials, ideal for pharmaceuticals and food products. Manifold freeze dryers function by freezing and sublimating multiple small samples simultaneously, suitable for laboratory use. Rotary freeze dryers incorporate rotating chambers to maximize surface area and enhance drying efficiency, often employed in research. Other types may include vacuum tunnel dryers or customized solutions for specific industry needs.</p></p>
