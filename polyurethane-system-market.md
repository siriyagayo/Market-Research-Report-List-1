<p><p>The polyurethane system market is primarily categorized into two types: thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic polyurethanes (TPUs) are versatile, allowing for reshaping upon heating, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility and durability. Conversely, thermosetting polyurethanes are characterized by their permanent structure after curing, offering enhanced heat resistance and strength, suitable for rigid applications like coatings, adhesives, and foams. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and furniture, driving demand through their unique properties.</p></p>
