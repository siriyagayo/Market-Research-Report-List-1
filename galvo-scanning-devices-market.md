<p><p>Galvo scanning devices are categorized into three market types based on their movement capabilities: single-axis, dual-axis, and triaxis. </p><p>Single-axis devices move along one plane, primarily used for basic applications like simple laser scanning. Dual-axis devices allow movement on two planes, enhancing versatility for more complex tasks, such as in laser engraving and marking. Triaxis devices provide three-dimensional movement, ideal for advanced applications like 3D scanning and high-precision laser machining, offering greater agility and accuracy for intricate projects.</p></p>
