<p><p>The manual hand sanitizer dispensers market is primarily categorized into wall-mounted and counter-mounted top-fill dispensers. Wall-mounted hand sanitizer dispensers are designed for fixed installation on walls, providing convenient access in high-traffic areas like offices and public spaces. Both types emphasize hygiene and accessibility in various environments.</p></p>
