<p><p>The surgical bioadhesive market encompasses various types, primarily including fibrin-based adhesives, mussel adhesive proteins, and others. Fibrin adhesives mimic natural clotting, promoting wound healing and tissue adhesion. Mussel adhesive proteins, derived from marine organisms, provide strong bonding in wet conditions, ideal for challenging surgical environments. Each type is tailored to enhance surgical outcomes and facilitate tissue integration effectively.</p></p>
